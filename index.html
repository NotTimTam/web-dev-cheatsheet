    ABOUT

    ASTRONEER

    A GAME OF AEROSPACE INDUSTRY AND INTERPLANETARY EXPLORATION

    Astroneer is set during the 25th century’s Intergalactic Age of Discovery, where Astroneers explore the frontiers of outer space, risking their lives in harsh environments to unearth rare discoveries and unlock the mysteries of the universe. Players can work together to build custom bases and vehicles and use terrain to create anything they can imagine. A player’s creativity and ingenuity are the key to thriving on exciting planetary adventures!

    QUICK HELP

    ASTRONEER BASICS

    CONTROLS

    Traverse alien worlds.

    WALK | W A S D

    RUN | (HOLD) SHIFT

    JUMP | SPACE BAR

    View your surroundings.

    ORBIT | RIGHT MOUSE BUTTON

    ZOOM | MOUSE SCROLL WHEEL

    Find strange and interesting objects.

    CURSOR | (HOLD) LEFT MOUSE BUTTON

    PICK UP/DROP | (CLICK) LEFT MOUSE BUTTON

    QUICK STOW | PICK UP + F or SHIFT + LEFT MOUSE BUTTON

    INTERACTION

    EXAMINE reveals more options for interaction with objects like control panels.

    EXAMINE | F

    USE objects to immediately activate them

    USE | F

    AUXILIARY SLOTS on the Backpack and Vehicles can act as additional storage or hold useful items such as Generators and Drills.

    ACTIVATE | C and V

    TERRAIN TOOL

    Equip your TERRAIN TOOL to reshape the environment.

    EQUIP TOOL | E

    DIG | LEFT MOUSE BUTTON

    With a CANISTER equipped, you can use the Terrain Tool to flatten and add terrain.

    ADD | ALT + LEFT MOUSE BUTTON

    FLATTEN | CTRL + LEFT MOUSE BUTTON

    Use the Terrain Tool to excavate RESOURCES. These can be used to create many different things.

    Attaching AUGMENTS to the Terrain Tool will enhance its performance and help deform through Hard Terrain.

    INVENTORY

    Your BACKPACK shows your OXYGEN and POWER, and holds your Inventory.

    BACKPACK | Q

    Your Backpack also holds a PRINTER, which can create Small Items.

    Craft and place TETHERS to carry Oxygen and Power away from your SHELTER and expand your area of exploration.

    TETHER | T

    Vehicles and Shelters provide oxygen, but require an OXYGENATOR to support Tether networks..
